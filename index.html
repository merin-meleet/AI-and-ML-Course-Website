Welcome to the Course

This course provides an introduction to the field of Artificial Intelligence (AI). We will cover fundamental concepts, algorithms, and applications of AI, including machine learning, neural networks, and ethical considerations.

Whether you're a student enrolled in the course or an interested visitor, you'll find useful information here, including the syllabus, weekly schedule, assignments, and additional resources.

Syllabus

Course Objectives

  • Understand the history and foundations of AI.
  • Learn key AI techniques such as search algorithms, knowledge representation, and machine learning.
  • Explore real-world applications and ethical issues in AI.

Grading Policy
